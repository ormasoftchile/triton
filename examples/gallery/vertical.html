Vertical-Spine Layout Family

The VERTICAL CENTRAL-SPINE family places the time axis top→bottom with a central spine line. Entries alternate left and right, connected by short horizontal connectors. Card-oriented themes (product, executive) render rounded-rect entry blocks; plain themes (consulting, minimal) render clean text-only entries. All renders are deterministic — sceneHash is stable across runs.

layout=vertical-spine T1 / T3 / T5 targets deterministic SVG + PNG
